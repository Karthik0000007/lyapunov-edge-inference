## ARCHITECTURE (45 sec)
*[Let video play]*

"The key innovation is the **three-layer adaptive controller**:

**Layer 1**: **Lyapunov-PPO agent** — makes decisions in under 0.1 milliseconds, selecting from 27 configurations trading quality for latency. Lyapunov constraint ensures safety.

**Layer 2**: **Conformal predictor** — provides 99% confidence upper bound on latency, can override the RL agent to prevent violations. Adapts online to distribution shifts.

**Layer 3**: **Rule-based fallback** — deterministic safety net if learned layers fail.

Result: **Sub-10 millisecond inference** on most frames, P99 under 50ms, maintaining **over 80% baseline accuracy**."
